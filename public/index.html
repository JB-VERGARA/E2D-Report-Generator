Inspection Report Generator

How to Use the System

Welcome to the Inspection Report Generator! Follow these simple steps to generate your reports:

  1. Upload your Excel file: Choose the Excel file that contains the necessary data.
  2. Select document template: Choose between "1st Border" or "2nd Border" to apply to your reports.
  3. Click the "Generate Reports" button: The system will automatically generate a ZIP file with your reports.
  4. Download your reports: Once the ZIP file is ready, you can download it and view your reports in Word format.
